Fuji

UI/UX Design 2025 Figma

The Challenge

"The Fuji required a digital presence that reflected their minimalist architectural philosophy. The existing site was cluttered and difficult to navigate on mobile devices."

My goal was to strip away the non-essential elements and focus purely on visual storytelling.

The Solution

I developed a design system based on a 12-column grid. Typography was kept stark and monochromatic to ensure the photography took center stage.

The result was a 40% increase in user session duration.

Architecture that lasts. We see a world where buildings are valued for their lasting impact, and craftsmanship and materials are respected.
